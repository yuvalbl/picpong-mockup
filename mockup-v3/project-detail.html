The build

Branded, lightweight, and gone without a trace.

For Microsoft's new-office launch we built the whole welcome environment from X-Board — direct-printed panels, free-standing structures and counters that carry in by hand, assemble tool-free, and hold their edge under a full day of foot traffic.

When the launch wrapped, none of it went to landfill. The panels fold flat for storage and come back out for the next event — or get recycled. Impact on the floor; nothing left behind.

  • Direct-printed X-Board, no laminates
  • Carried in, assembled tool-free on site
  • Struck down, stored flat, reused

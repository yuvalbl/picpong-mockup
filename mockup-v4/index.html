Why cardboard

Impact on the floor. None on the planet.

X-Board is a very dense, environmentally friendly panel made of 100% recycled cardboard. Picpong is its exclusive importer, and we take it from planning and design through printing and installation, all in-house.

  • Print directly onto the panel: no laminates, no waste
  • Lightweight to ship, fast to assemble on site
  • Disassemble, store flat, and reuse for the next event
  • Fully recyclable at end of life
